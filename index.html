Short Story

One bright spring morning, Benny the Bunny, Clara the Cow, and Penny the Pig lined up at the edge of the meadow. Today was the Great Garden Race! Each friend had a basket of seeds and a patch of land to plant. Benny bounced to his spot, planting quickly with his tiny paws. Clara trotted over, placing each seed carefully in neat rows. Penny twirled and giggled, tossing seeds with flair and joy. As the sun climbed higher, the friends worked hard, helping each other and sharing laughs.
